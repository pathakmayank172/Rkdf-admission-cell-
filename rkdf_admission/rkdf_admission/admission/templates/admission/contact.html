Q: When do admissions open?

Admissions for the 2026-27 academic year are open from April 1, 2026. The last date to apply is June 30, 2026.

Q: Is there an entrance exam?

Most programs admit students on the basis of merit. However, some programs like MBA may require an entrance test. Contact us for details.

Q: What documents are needed for admission?

10th & 12th mark sheets, transfer certificate, migration certificate, character certificate, Aadhaar card, passport-size photographs, and caste certificate (if applicable).

Q: Is scholarship available?

Yes! Merit scholarships are available for students with 70%+ marks in the qualifying exam. Government scholarships (SC/ST/OBC/EWS) are also applicable.

Q: Is hostel facility available?

Yes, separate hostels for boys and girls are available on campus with mess, Wi-Fi, and security. Hostel allocation is subject to availability.
