A Legacy of Academic Excellence

RKDF University Ranchi is a premier private university located in Namkum, Ranchi, Jharkhand. Established under the RKDF Group of Institutions — a 30-year legacy of educational excellence — the university stands as a beacon of quality education in the eastern region of India.

The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across Engineering, Management, Science, Commerce, Law, Pharmacy and Arts. With a UGC-recognized and NAAC-accredited status, RKDF University provides globally competitive education right here in Jharkhand.
